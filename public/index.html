Horacio Moreno Montañes

I am Applied and Interdisciplinary Mathematics PhD student at the University of Michigan. I am interested in exploring mathematical patterns embedded in physical phenomena and using them so solve real-world probems. My current interests are the mathematical modeling and both the design and implementation of numerical methods for problems in fluid and plasma dynamics. I am also interested in exploring the application of machine learning and neural networks to physics problems.
